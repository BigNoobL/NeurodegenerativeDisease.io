Alzheimer's Disease

Curriculum

Alzheimer's gradually damages memory and thinking. Brain cells die over time, making it hard to remember recent events, names, and conversations. Mental exercises help keep your brain active.

Curriculum

Parkinson's affects movement and coordination due to dopamine loss. Exercises that train rhythm, coordination, and reaction can support motor control.
